Hand History Viewer

Help
  • Go to "My Sky Poker" then "Hand History" to find the hand you want to share
  • Click "Copy" in the popup
  • Paste it into the box above and click "View"
  • Click "Clear" to empty the box and enter a new hand
Tools
Tips
  • Click a villain's name to anonymise them
  • Click "Convert to BB" to convert chip values to big blinds
  • Click "Save as image" to download an image of the hand
  • Get help or suggest an improvement in this forum thread
